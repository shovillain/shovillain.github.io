Codeine aka 3-methylmorphine.


Formula C18 H21 N03

Codeine was discovered in 1832.[6] In 2013 about 361,000 kilograms of codeine were produced while 249,000 kilograms were used.

Codeine is also available in conjunction with the anti-nausea medication promethazine in the form of a syrup. Brand named as Phenergan with Codeine or in generic form as promethazine with codeine. Called 'syrup', 'lean', or 'purple drank', this medication is quickly becoming one of the most commonly misused codeine preparations
